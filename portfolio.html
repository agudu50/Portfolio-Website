About Me

Hi, I’m Anthony Gudu, a passionate Frontend Developer and aspiring Full-Stack Engineer. I enjoy creating clean, modern, and user-friendly web applications with a focus on responsive design and smooth user experiences.

Currently, I’m pursuing a BSc in Computer Science at the University of Ghana. Over the years, I’ve built projects ranging from management systems to portfolio websites, applying technologies like Java, Python, PHP, C#, ASP.NET MVC, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I’m also deeply interested in telecommunications, AI, and networking, and I enjoy tackling challenges that combine problem-solving with creativity. My goal is to grow into a well-rounded software engineer who builds impactful solutions.
